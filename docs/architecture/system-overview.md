# Trading-RnD-AI System Overview

## Purpose

Trading-RnD-AI is an AI-assisted research and development platform for algorithmic trading.

The platform is designed to collect knowledge from multiple trusted sources, transform that knowledge into structured research assets, generate trading products, validate them through automated experimentation, and continuously improve them using evidence-based reasoning.

The long-term vision is to build a research platform rather than a single Expert Advisor.

---

## Core Principles

- Rule-Based First
- AI-Assisted Decision Making
- Evidence Before Knowledge
- Technology Independent Core
- Plugin-Based Architecture
- Reproducible Research
- Full Provenance Tracking

## Primary Goals

- Build algorithmic trading knowledge.
- Generate high-quality trading products.
- Validate every generated strategy.
- Preserve scientific evidence.
- Support continuous learning.

---

## Non Goals

The platform does not aim to:

- Guarantee profitable trading.
- Replace human judgment.
- Execute uncontrolled autonomous trading.
- Ignore licensing or provenance.
